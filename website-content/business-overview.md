# Lightpath Property Solutions - Business Overview

## Company Description

Lightpath Property Solutions specializes in solar energy solutions for commercial properties. The company provides innovative, zero-capex solar solutions that enable landlords to generate rental income while reducing operational costs.

## Key Business Model

- **Zero-Capex Solutions**: No upfront investment required from property owners
- **Rental Income Generation**: Landlords can earn income from their roof space
- **Operational Cost Reduction**: Lower energy costs for commercial properties
- **Fully Funded Projects**: Complete financing solutions provided

## Target Market

- Commercial property owners
- Retail centres and shopping complexes
- Industrial facilities
- Office buildings

## Case Study Example

**Modern Retail Centre Project**: A fully operational rooftop solar system installation demonstrating how landlords can generate rental income and reduce operational costs through zero-capex solar solutions.

## Services Offered

1. **Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs)**
2. **Cash Purchase Systems**
3. **Roof Rental Models**
4. **Project Management and Installation**
5. **Ongoing Maintenance and Support**

## Geographic Focus

Primary operations in South Africa, with headquarters in Johannesburg.
